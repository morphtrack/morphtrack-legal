# Morph account deletion

You can delete your Morph account and all associated data directly in the app: open Profile → tap Delete account and confirm.

When you delete your account, we delete:

- your account information (email, display name, password hash)
- all photos you have uploaded, including their thumbnails — both the image files in our storage and the database records that reference them
- all app data associated with your account
  
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You do not need to email us — the in‑app action triggers full deletion on our servers.

Some data may be retained for a limited period if required for security, fraud prevention, legal, or operational reasons.

If you can no longer sign in and need help deleting your account, contact us at morphtrack.app@gmail.com and include the email address linked to your Morph account.

# Photo deletion
You can delete an individual photo at any time from inside the app.

When you delete a photo, we delete:

- the original image file from our storage
- the generated thumbnail of that image from our storage
- the database record that linked the photo to your account

Photo deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Other photos in your account are not affected.
